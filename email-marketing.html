The Juicy Crab Dothan Case Study How we planned and executed
A successful opening

Client’s proposal

The Juicy Crab, a chain seafood restaurant company reached out to Best POS and began describing the experience of decreasing the deliverability rate, declining open rates, high bounce rates, etc. They wanted to scale up their email marketing campaigns and increase revenue by reclaiming opportunities lost due to mail delivery problems. They choose the “Email Marketing” package from Best POS to solve these issues which included Weekly/Monthly Newsletters, Announcement Email Campaigns, and Promotional or Event-based Email Campaigns.

Best POS Solutions

Best POS conducted a deliverability audit of The Juicy Crab's current email practices and uncovered several impediments that negatively impact deliverability. Best POS analyzed many technical and creative factors used in email campaigns and determined the corrective actions needed to deliver consistently to target users' inboxes.

The Best POS audit team uncovered that their emails were going to the spam folder in order to have spammy words in the email body, and they had broken DNS records that negatively affected their deliverability rate. In order to recover the delivery issues and increase the open rates and conversions, necessary steps were made by the Best POS email marketing team such as solving DNS issues, removing spammy words, improving quality content, using a subject line that has great open rates, proper email list segmentation, and consistent monitoring of email campaigns. These changes were made to ensure that all emails are delivered to The Juicy Crab’s customer primary inboxes and its increase in the open rates, clicks, and sales.

Results
Email Marketing
  • 99.99% primary inbox placement & 0.8%Bounce rate
  • 0.1% of Average record & Open rate of over 45%
  • 26%+ Conversion rate

The advice and knowledge of Best POS' experienced strategists have greatly
enhanced The Juicy Crab's reputation as a sender and inbox placement.
